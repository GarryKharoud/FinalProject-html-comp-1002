Welcome To Playstation Online

There are two games you can play

The Instructions for Rock Paper Scissor's are :

  • 1. This is a paper scissors rock game created using AI.
  • 2. The rules of game are similar to a normal Rock Paper game
  • 3. You can start game by selecting "rock","scissors" or "paper".
  • 4. The Computer will then Randomly choose its option

The Instructions for Guessing game are :

  • 1. This is a guessing game, you will be given a riddle.
  • 2. You will be given three chances to guess correctly.
  • 3. Press Start Game to get a riddle and check to see you guessed
    correctly
  • 4. Press Newbutton for a new question after all chances are gone
    or you guessed incorrectly
  • 5. You can spell upper or lowercase
